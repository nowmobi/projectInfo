Introduction

We highly value user privacy protection and strictly safeguard the security of your personal information.

I. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy aims to explain how this financial information platform collects, uses, stores, protects, and discloses users' personal information. This policy applies to all users who access and use this website, related services, and features. By accessing, browsing, and using the platform services, you indicate your full agreement with all terms of this Privacy Policy. We will follow the principles of legality, legitimacy, necessity, and transparency to protect the legitimate rights and interests of your personal information.

II. Information We Collect

To provide users with more accurate, personalized, and secure financial information services, we collect a small amount of necessary information from voluntarily registered users. The specific collection types are as follows:

  • Behavioral data: Including your browsing history, reading preferences, search content, page stay duration, and interactive behaviors on the platform, used to optimize content recommendations and enhance user experience.
  • Device information: Including device model, operating system, network environment, IP address, etc., used for platform security monitoring, risk prevention, and abnormal access identification.

Without your explicit authorization, we will not actively collect sensitive financial privacy information such as your bank account, payment password, and asset details.

III. How We Use Your Information

All user information we collect is used solely for legitimate service purposes. We promise not to use it for any unauthorized scenarios. The main usage scope includes:

  • Provide, maintain, and optimize core platform services such as financial information query, content browsing, and news push;
  • Conduct user identity verification and account security management to prevent fraudulent access and irregular operations;
  • Analyze platform operation data and user demand trends, iterate and upgrade product functions, and optimize service quality;
  • Push necessary service notifications, system updates, and rule reminders;
  • Comply with national laws, regulations, and regulatory requirements, and respond to official legal inquiries and instructions.

IV. Information Storage and Security Protection

We adopt industry-proven technical means and management systems to comprehensively protect the security of your personal information. User data is stored in encrypted form, with strict access control, regular data backup, and security inspection mechanisms to effectively prevent risks of information leakage, tampering, loss, and abuse.

We only retain user information for the period required by services and legal regulations. When information no longer needs to be retained, we will completely delete it or anonymize it through secure technical means. For user browsing behavior data, we will implement a regular cleaning mechanism to reduce data storage security risks.

V. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Without your explicit consent, we will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. We may disclose user information in compliance with regulations only in the following limited scenarios:

  • With your voluntary authorization and confirmation;
  • Pursuant to national laws, regulations, judicial judgments, and mandatory requirements of administrative law enforcement agencies;
  • To protect the legitimate rights and interests of the platform, users, and public safety, such as preventing fraud, cyber attacks, and other illegal activities.

VI. User Rights

You have complete rights over your personal information, including the right to query, view, correct, delete, withdraw authorization, and cancel your account. If you need to exercise the above rights, you can contact us through the platform's official customer service channel, and we will complete the response and processing within the time limit specified by law.

VII. Cookie Usage Rules

This platform uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to optimize your browsing experience. Cookies can record your browsing preferences and access status, helping you quickly log in to your account and receive accurate financial content push. You can manage or disable cookies through browser settings at any time, but disabling them may cause some platform functions to not work properly.

VIII. Policy Updates

We will revise this Privacy Policy from time to time according to platform service upgrades, business adjustments, and updates to laws and regulations. After the policy is updated, we will publish the latest version on the platform's homepage, and the updated content will take effect from the date of publication. Your continued use of the platform services shall be deemed as recognition and acceptance of the updated policy terms.

IX. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy and personal information protection, you can contact the official customer service team through the platform's built-in message channel, and we will answer and handle them for you as soon as possible.
